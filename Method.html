Method

  1. Prepare five cups (paper, plastic, glass, styrofoam, ceramic) and cover them with foil.
  2. Use a funnel to make a hole for pouring water and inserting a thermometer.
  3. Boil 100 ml of water using a kettle.
  4. Pour water into each cup, insert thermometers, and begin timing from 70°C.
  5. Record the time taken for each to cool to 39°C.
